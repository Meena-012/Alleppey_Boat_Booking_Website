Alleppey Boathouse Food Menu

Delicacies of Kerala are filled with aromatic flavors you just cant resist. Our food menu varies slightly for delux houseboats, premium houseboats and luxury houseboats. One thing is for sure, with every morsel you will only want more.

Deluxe Boathouse Food Menu

Welcome Drink

Tender Coconut Juice or Fresh Juice

LUNCH

The meal is a vibrant mix of local delicacies, highlighting freshly caught seafood, often prepared as fish curry or succulent prawn dishes. Alongside, there are aromatic rice dishes, lentils, and an assortment of vegetable curries, including the region's famous avial and thoran. Infused with coconut, curry leaves, and an array of spices, each dish showcases Kerala's traditional cooking techniques. Served amidst the serene backwaters.

EVENING SNACKS

These snacks often include crispy banana chips, crunchy tapioca fries, and spicy fish cutlets, showcasing Kerala's love for seafood. The menu may feature savory items like 'pakoras' made from fresh vegetables and 'vadas' (deep-fried lentil fritters). This culinary journey through Alleppey's evening snacks creates a tasty and culturally immersive experience amidst the tranquil backwaters.

DINNER

Accompanying these mains are fluffy appams or steamed rice, providing a perfect base to savor the complex flavors of the region. The dinner spread often incorporates traditional accompaniments like 'thoran' (stir-fried vegetables with coconut) and 'rasam' (a tangy soup), concluding with sweet treats like 'payasam' (a creamy rice pudding)

BREAKFAST

It typically features a spread of local specialties, including 'appam,' lacy rice pancakes served with coconut milk or stew, and 'puttu,' cylindrical steamed rice cakes paired with banana or grated coconut. Accompanying these staples are dishes like 'idli' (steamed rice cakes), 'dosa' (thin crepes), and a variety of chutneys and sambar. Fresh fruits, including bananas and pineapples, add a refreshing touch.
